Notation

Drum rudiments are written using the percussion clef. Here are the pieces you will see on each rudiment's notation.

Percussion Clef

The two vertical bars at the start of the staff identify it as a percussion staff (used for unpitched instruments like the snare drum).

Eighth Note

Each filled note head with a flag (or beam) represents one stroke. Rudiments in this course use eighth notes, beamed together in groups of four.

Right Hand (R)

An "R" beneath a note means you play that stroke with your right hand.

Left Hand (L)

An "L" beneath a note means you play that stroke with your left hand.
